When all you have is a hammer

Weekly: Thu, 11 May 2017

Featured

Deep Learning’s Impact on Image Processing, Mathematics, and Humanity
sinews.siam.org

A reflection on the consequences of having one tool in your box that starts to outperform every other tool. If your hammer can do everything, often better than anything else, eventually we stop bothering to make other tools. The author laments the effect of the ascent of Neural Networks as an intractable “hammer” that has transformed the research landscape, and not necessarily for the best. Are we sacrificing broad academic exploration to a new golden calf? Maybe.
