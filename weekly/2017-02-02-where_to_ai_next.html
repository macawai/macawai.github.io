Performance Trends in AI
srconstantin.wordpress.com

So just how good is AI? More importantly, how much better is Deep Learning than the algorithms we were using to solve those problems beforehand (and how is that related to hardware performance gains)? This article looks at games, image recognition, speech recognition, machine translation and natural language processing to draw a tentative conclusion that Deep Learning does indeed offer discontinuous improvement for specific, narrow domains (image and speech recognition), however does not seem to do the same for symbolic or general problem domains.
