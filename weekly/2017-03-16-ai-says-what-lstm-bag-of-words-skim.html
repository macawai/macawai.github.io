Featured

Learning when to skim and when to read
metamind.io

“Do we always need human level accuracy in real world data?” - well, no. And that’s the point this awesome article on NLP techniques and approaches. Can we separate the ‘easy’ vs. ‘hard’ sentences and use cheap models to do so? And more formally, “Can we learn when reading is strictly better than skimming in a supervised way?”. By tuning confidence values and avoiding running expensive LSTM, it looks like we can trade computation time vs accuracy without necessarily sacrificing performance. Some great interactive visualisations help drive the argument.
