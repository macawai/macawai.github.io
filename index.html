Weekly: Thu, 9 Feb 2017

Featured

The most dangerous AI - Political bias in artificial intelligence algorithms
blog.plan99.net

“Sci-fi AI usually tries to change the world using weapons, and we are all highly alert to the dangers of armed autonomous robots. It rarely shows AI trying to change the world by manipulating the answers it gives to people’s questions.” What does it mean when AI returns word embedding associations that are politically loaded. What does it mean when we deliberately try to change this? Who decides if what should change and why? This article looks at some of the issues around the sometimes uncomfortable mirror that AI-learning-from-us can present: “This kind of research is both scary and dangerous.Scary because regardless of how you may want the world to be, the learnings from bulk text feeds are as close as we can realistically get to how the world actually is. Dangerous because.. software should not fight culture wars”

See the related genesis paper “Man is to Computer Programmer as Woman is to Homemaker? Debiasing Word Embeddings”.
